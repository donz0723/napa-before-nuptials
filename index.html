Getting Here

Find Your Way

The Bay Area is served by three major airports. Here's everything you need to pick the right one.

SFO
San Francisco International
The main hub — the most airline options, including international routes. BART connects you to downtown in ~25 minutes for $10, or take a rideshare.
Most Convenient
OAK
Oakland International
Just across the bay. Often has lower fares — particularly on Southwest. About 35–45 minutes to downtown SF by BART or rideshare.
SJC
San Jose International
About 50–60 minutes south of the city. Worth checking if fares are meaningfully lower — smaller airport, lighter crowds.
BART
Fast, affordable, and reliable from SFO and OAK directly into the city. No traffic, no guesswork.
Rideshare
Uber and Lyft are everywhere in the Bay Area — ideal if you have luggage or are arriving late.
On Foot
San Francisco is wonderfully walkable. Once you're checked in, many of our spots will be steps away.
Napa Day
A shuttle will pick up from the hotel Friday morning and bring everyone back in the evening — no car rental needed.

Before You Pack

Good to Know

A few things worth knowing before you arrive.

01
The Weather
August in San Francisco can be cooler and foggier than you'd expect — especially mornings and evenings. Napa will be hot (often 90°F+). Pack layers for the city and light breezy clothes for the valley.
02
What to Bring
Comfortable walking shoes are essential — SF is hilly. A light jacket for SF evenings, something comfortable for Napa's warmth, and yoga clothes for Sunday morning.
03
Book Early
August is peak season across the Bay Area. Flights and accommodations fill up quickly — lock in your travel as soon as possible to get the best options.
04
Napa Day
Summer Napa is magical. We'll handle the winery reservations and group logistics — just come ready for a beautiful day in wine country.
05
Budget
A full breakdown of costs will be shared closer to the trip. Napa tastings and group activities may have a small contribution — we'll keep it fair and transparent.
06
Group Chat
We'll set up a group chat closer to the date for real-time coordination, arrival updates, and all the anticipatory excitement.
